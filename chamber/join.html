Membership Tiers

NP

This tier provides basic membership for non profit organizations and there is no fee. Benefits include:

  • Your own page on the Chamber website with a link to your business website or Facebook page
  • Direct Member-to-Member advertising through the Chamber website.
  • Member Directory listing.
  • Free business card and brochure display at Chamber.

Bronze

This tier provides basic membership for standard businesses and costs $399.00 USD annually. Benefits include:

  • Your own page on the Chamber website with a link to your business website or Facebook page
  • Direct Member-to-Member advertising through the Chamber website.
  • Member Directory listing.
  • Free business card and brochure display at Chamber.
  • Opportunity to advertise on the Chamber’s website.

Silver

This tier provides premium membership for businesses and costs $1,500.00 USD annually. Benefits include all of the benefits listed for Bronze Membership, PLUS:

  • Free “Hot Deal” Promotions on our website.
  • Free participation in our Chamber Check Program to increase your sales and keep money local
  • Promotional materials in golf outing goody bags.
  • Ribbon cuttings.
  • Professional business referrals

Gold

This tier provides ultimate membership for businesses and costs $2,500.00 USD annually. Benefits include all of the benefits listed for Bronze and Silver Memberships, PLUS:

  • Business and legislative advocacy services
  • Free “Behind the Mic” radio spot promos every 2nd & 4th Friday.
  • Professional Development workshop luncheons
  • Mentoring, training and workshops on a variety of topics (provided through a partnership with SCORE and the Better Business Bureau)
  • Networking opportunities
  • Discounts to all Chamber-sponsored events
  • Free use of the Chamber conference room for meetings and events
  • PHP Healthcare Discount! Employers can save up to 30% over ACA Plans!
  • Member-to-Member discount program
